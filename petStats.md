## **Pets have the following stats: **

- type (random number between 1 and 10, which will determine their visual look in the frontend)
- hunger (max 100)
- happiness (max 100)
- health (max 100)
- energy (max 100)

## **Pets can perform the following actions:**

- feed (+10 to hunger)
- pet (+5 to happiness)
- play (-5 to energy and +10 to happiness)
- heal (+10 to health)
- rest (+10 to energy)
